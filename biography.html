--- layout: default title: Bruno Dayan - Biography ---

Born in London, England, raised in Montreal, Canada.

Studies Fine Arts and Cinema at Concordia University in Montreal.
Second year student short filmÂ selected at Cannes Film Festival.

1982 - Starts photographic career in Toronto. Shoots for main Canadian fashion magazines.

Starts traveling extensively in Asia.
1986 - Moves to Tokyo.
Shoots editorials, videos and commercials for top Japanese music talents while continuing fashion photography.

1997 - Moves to Paris.
Shoots campaigns for Louis Vuitton, Yves St Laurent, Dior, Moschino, Celine, Escada, Swarovski, Chantal Thomass, Le Printemps, Le Bon Marché, Omega, ...

2013 - Solo exhibition "A Corps Perdu" at Elephant Paname in Paris
Group Exhibition at Contributed Gallery in Berlin
Published in Prestel "New Fashion Photography"
Group Exhibition "Strangeness" at Atelier TOZF Gallerie in Paris

2014 - Moves to New York.
Shoots campaigns for Absolut Vodka, Vera Wang, Waldorf Astoria, ...

Has been contributing to publications such as Numéro, Vogue, Vanity Fair, Harper's Bazaar, Marie Claire, Elle, ...

Currently based in New York.
